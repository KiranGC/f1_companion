# Privacy Policy

**F1 Companion** (the "App") is a free mobile application designed to show Formula 1 live countdowns, weekend schedules, and race replays.

## 1. Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage statistics, device logs, location data, or identifiers.
- **No User Accounts**: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal details (like name, email, or phone number) to use the App.
- **Telemetry Data**: The App acts purely as a client interface to display public Formula 1 timing and telemetry data retrieved from open APIs. This telemetry data is never tracked, stored on servers, or associated with your device or identity.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App fetches race telemetry and schedules from the following public API sources:
- **OpenF1 API** (Public race telemetry, interval data, and control flags).
- **Jolpica/Ergast API** (Race calendar, schedules, and standings).
These APIs are queried directly from your device. We do not control or operate these third-party endpoints, and their usage is subject to their respective terms. No personal telemetry is shared with these APIs.

## 3. Storage & Device Permissions
- **Local Cache**: The App caches session details locally on your device (such as loaded race positions and circuit configurations) for the duration of the playback session. This data is temporary and remains entirely local to your device.
- **Internet Access**: The App requires network access (`android.permission.INTERNET`) to fetch real-time race telemetry.

## 4. Children's Privacy
The App does not collect any personal data, making it safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the policy file directly in this public repository.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, feel free to open an issue or contact the repository owner on GitHub.
